Espresso
A strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing pressurized water through finely ground coffee beans. Buy now
Doppio
Doppio espresso is a double shot which is extracted using double the amount of ground coffee in a larger-sized portafilter basket. Buy now
Americano
A single shot of espresso added to a cup of hot water. Many like to drink this as they would a regular brewed coffee with milk and sugar. Buy now
Ristretto
Ristretto short shot of espresso, meaning barista pulls first portion of a full-length espresso shot. Buy now
Lungo
A lungo is a shot of espresso made with more water. It’s a bigger drink than espresso shot because more water runs through the coffee. Buy now
Freddo
Freddo Espresso is the cold version of espresso coffee, made with a double shot of espresso Buy now
Romano
Espresso romano is a shot of espresso with a squeeze of lemon and a lemon wedge on the top. Buy now
Honey Raf
Honey raf coffee necessarily contains honey, while classic raf is sweetened with sugar. Buy now

Coffee

A coffee house is a subspecies of a cafe, a gastronomic establishment, a place for meetings and communication, where drinks and dessert dishes are served: coffee, cakes, ice cream, green and black tea, juices, as well as carbonated and alcoholic drinks. Many coffee shops in the Middle East and West Asia also offer shisha and flavored tobacco.
